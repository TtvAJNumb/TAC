Who we are

Southern California's A/C equipment specialists

Turiz A/C Equipment Service is a dedicated repair and calibration shop for professional automotive A/C machines. We work on and other leading brands — keeping dealerships and independent shops up and running.

  • Vacuum pump oil service & compressor oil flush
  • Gauge, scale & transducer calibration
  • Filter replacement & maintenance defaults reset
  • Full function & leak testing with certification
  • Hoses, couplers, cords, wheels & accessories
  • Convenient pick-up & delivery service
  • Free R-134 loaner units available while we service yours
What we do

Specialized A/C equipment service

From vacuum pump maintenance to full unit overhauls, we keep your professional A/C equipment running right.

Vacuum pump service
Oil drain flush & refill to maintain pump efficiency and extend lifespan.
Gauge & valve calibration
Calibrate gauges, expansion valves, scales, and transducers for precise readings.
Filter & defaults reset
Filter replacement and vacuum pump maintenance defaults restored to spec.
Function & leak test
Full functional testing, leak verification, and certification sticker on completion.
Pick-up & delivery
We come to you. No need to haul your machine — we handle logistics.
Parts & accessories
Service hoses, couplers, power cords, wheels, tank straps, and certified components.
Free R-134 loaners
When available, we lend loaner R-134 units at no extra cost so your shop stays up and running.
